# Sound Journey

*A meditative, immersive sound experience designed for deep listening, relaxation, and nervous-system regulation.*

A sitar-led, participatory sound journey blending **improvisation**, **meditative listening**, and **storytelling** _ guiding audiences through stillness, depth, rhythm, and release.

- Adaptable in intensity: from deeply meditative to gently rhythmic, depending on the setting

- **Solo**
- **With percussion or additional musicians** (by request)
- Can be tailored to theme, space, and audience
