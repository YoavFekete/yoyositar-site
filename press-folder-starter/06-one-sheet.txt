Short bio
YoYo Sitar is a contemporary sitar artist creating spacious, emotionally direct music rooted in Indian raga and shaped by cross-cultural depth, improvisation, and modern groove. His performances move fluidly between intimate listening settings and festival stages, with current work spanning solo and collaborative formats alongside a debut EP scheduled for 2026.
