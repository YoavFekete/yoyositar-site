YoYo Sitar (Yoav Fekete) is a contemporary sitar artist blending Indian raga, West African string traditions, and modern groove into a spacious and emotionally direct sound. Trained in Indian classical music under Rudra Veena master Dudu Elkabir of the Dagar Gharana lineage, his playing carries the depth of the dhrupad tradition while moving fluidly across cultures. His debut EP, scheduled for release in 2026, explores the meeting point between raga, African melody, and contemporary rhythm.
